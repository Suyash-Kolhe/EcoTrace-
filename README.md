# 🌍 EcoTrace

**Action-Led Decarbonization Guide**

EcoTrace is a carbon footprint tracking and reduction app that goes beyond simple calculation — it turns awareness into action through habit pledges, AI-assisted projections, contextual green swaps, and verified carbon offsetting.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Dashboard
Real-time overview of your active carbon pledge, category breakdown (Transportation, Home Energy, Diet & Food, Sourcing & Shopping), and weekly carbon velocity tracking — all benchmarked against Paris Agreement 1.5°C alignment thresholds.

### 🧮 Carbon Emissions Calculator
Step-by-step audit across four lifestyle segments:
- **Transport** — Petrol/Diesel/Electric vehicle km, bus, flights
- **Energy Footprint** — power, heating, oil
- **Food & Diet Sourcing** — meat, organic foods, waste
- **Shopping & Waste** — garments, appliances, recycling

Each input updates emissions instantly using research-backed coefficients.

### 🏃 Manual Activity & Distance Log
Log daily trips (biking, walking, transit) and see live CO₂e savings calculated against gasoline-car-equivalent baselines.

### ✅ Habit Pledge Reduction Checklist
20 curated lifestyle commitments across Transport, Energy, Food, and Shopping, filterable by difficulty (Easy/Medium/Hard). Each pledge shows a quantified kg CO₂/yr impact and rolls up into your overall **Climate Action Pledge** score against a 30% baseline reduction goal.

### 📈 AI Projections & Climate Coach
- Stacked-area **Footprint Progress Track** showing emissions by category over time
- **Target Calibration** bar chart benchmarking your baseline and pledge against India, World, and EU averages, and the Paris 2030 target
- 12-month pledge savings projections (cumulative or monthly view)

### 🛍️ Eco Swaps Marketplace
Surfaces your highest-impact footprint category and recommends trust-scored, verified alternatives — not generic advertising. Includes an interactive swap simulator (e-bike commuting, plant-based meals, community renewable energy) with live projected savings.

### 🏅 Carbon Offsets Hub
For residual emissions that can't be eliminated, browse a portfolio of globally audited offset projects (Gold Standard & Verra certified) — agroforestry, biogas cookstoves, wind energy — with a real-time tonnage-to-cost calculator.

## 🖥️ Tech Highlights

- Modular category-based architecture (Transport / Energy / Food / Shopping)
- Data visualizations for trend tracking and benchmark comparison
- Dark mode support
